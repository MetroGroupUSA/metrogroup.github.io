Video Editor

About the position

As a member of MetroGroup's awesome editing team, you would be responsible for brainstorming, creating and transforming ideas into completed visual stories. You must be able to manipulate and edit raw material, ensure logical sequencing and consult with stakeholders throughout the production process. Your finished work will be the “voice” of the client, their products and their brand.

About you

You like to be hands-on, and get them dirty. You are not overwhelmed by problems. You consider the dozen ways in which you can solve them, and make a bold move to pick the one you feel will win the fight. You have a strong desire to learn. And most importantly - you have a keen interest in creating useful experiences for the people who want them.

Responsibilities

  • Project ownership throughout the entire editing process
  • Proactively understand client's goals while bringing their messaging to life
  • Manage teams, timelines and project budgets

What you must have

  • Expert level competency with Adobe Premiere CC
  • The desire to explore different ideas and concepts for visual elements  
  • Strong knowledge of professional video cameras and 4K equipment

Experience

5+ years of professional experience in video editing

 

Multimedia Producer

About the position

Manages all aspects of multimedia and video related projects – working closely with content contributors, stakeholders, multimedia developers and vendors to ensure projects are delivered on message and on schedule.

About you

You are innovative and have a proven ability to develop creative concepts and collaborate with team leaders in a fast-paced environment. You are also knowledgable with video and multimedia production standards, formats and distribution methods.

Responsibilities

  • Promote best-practices for production, project management, scheduling and decision-making
  • Drive excellence throughout project teams and ensure client branding is on-target
  • Demonstrate enthusiasm for taking on responsibility and establishing relationships with clients

What you must have

  • Extensive hands-on experience in producing interactive multimedia campaigns
  • Proven track record of driving project development in conjunction with global clients
  • Exceptional interpersonal, team building and communication skills  

Experience

6+ years experience in working with high profile clients in science and technology.

  • Have and continue to develop strong technical understanding of digital tools and current workflows
  • Accurately estimate time and resource needs and consistently meet project deadlines

What you must have

  • Expert-level experience with After Effects, Cinema 4D and Maya
  • Passion for information design and data visualization
  • Strong graphic and visual design sensibilities  

Experience

At least 5 years of experience in motion graphics development with an emphasis on design and user experience
